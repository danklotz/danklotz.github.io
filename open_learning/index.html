Open Learning

Here I share sources for autonomous learning on the internet. The idea is by and large inspired by Una Kravets.

In high school I had the epiphany that it is better to follow good teachers thengood topics. Throughout my studies I tried to follow that maxim as good as possible, but forgot about it until recently. Albeit my pursuits follow topics, I will try to keep this in mind for this list. I prefer to share videos/audio here and not the general sources that one will find anywhere.
