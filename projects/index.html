rrrCOS

R and Hydrology. My attempt at distilling useful functions and common workflows from working with the hydrological model COSERO. So far it comprises two packages coscos and visCOS.

  • coscos collects primitives and basis for the data-analysis tasks around COSERO.
  • visCOS is collection of analysis- and plotting-functions to be used conjunction with the hydrological COSERO (or other models).
