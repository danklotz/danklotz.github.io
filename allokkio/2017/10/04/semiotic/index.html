Semiotic

Elijah Meeks is very smart and very much datavis. He created Semiotic.

A data visualization framework for react. The most interesting thing for me is its way to explicitly showing the uncertainty of the data by rendering the plots and charts accordingly. Elijah wrote a very nice article which lays out his ideas. Worth reading.

For me the linked processing library, i.e. handy, still look even bit better, but seems to be less focused on data and the creation of fast and loose plots. There is also a thoughtful post about the use of sketchiness for visualizing uncertainty.
